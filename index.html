Meet Our Team

Ananya Sharma

Ananya Sharma is a master artisan specializing in intricate handloom textiles.Ananya has honed her craft over 15 years, creating exquisite sarees and shawls that reflect the traditional artistry of her ancestors. Her work has been featured in numerous craft exhibitions

Rajesh Patel

Rajesh Patel is a renowned potter with over 20 years of experience in the field of ceramics.Rajesh's creations are known for their unique textures and vibrant glazes, capturing the essence of Indian culture. His dedication to his craft has earned him multiple awards and a loyal following among collectors.

Meera Desai

Meera Desai is a skilled jewelry designer who has been crafting beautiful handmade pieces for the past 12 years. Her expertise lies in traditional Indian jewelry, particularly in the use of Kundan and Meenakari techniques.
